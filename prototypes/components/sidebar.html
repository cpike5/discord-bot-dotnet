Sidebar States Overview

Comprehensive demonstration of all navigation item states and variations.

State Specifications:

  • Default: White text at 80% opacity, transparent background
  • Hover: Full white text, 10% white background overlay
  • Active: Full white text, 15% white background, rose left border (3px)
  • With Badge: Count or notification indicator aligned to the right
  • Section Titles: 50% opacity, uppercase, smaller font size

Color Tokens Used:

  • Background: var(--surface-primary) / var(--color-slate) - #555B6E
  • Active Border: var(--color-primary) / var(--color-rose) - #DA4167
  • Text: var(--text-on-dark) - #FFFFFF
  • Width: var(--sidebar-width) - 200px
